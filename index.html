His Royal Highness Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz, Crown Prince, announced a strategy for developing the Games sector in Saudi Arabia :

  • The strategy includes pumping investments up to 142 billion Saudi riyals in four programs covering various types of investments in Games Sector.
  • Invest 2 billion riyals into various investments in entrepreneurship for innovative companies.
  • Investing in programs and infrastructure at the local and international levels.
  • Providing opportunities for training, education and entrepreneurship.
  • Attracting the international company to the Kingdom..
  • Establish 250 Electronic Games Comapnies in Saudi Arabia
